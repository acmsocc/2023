Local stuff – what to do in Santa Cruz?

  • Visit the Santa Cruz Boardwalk and ride the Giant Dipper.
  • Get a snack and walk the bluffs at lighthouse field.
  • Stand under the giant redwoods at Henry Cowell Redwoods in Felton.
  • Have a glass of wine at sunset on the municipal pier.
  • Shop and get your feet wet at the seaside village of Capitola.
  • Play disc golf at De Laveaga park, where the sport was invented.
  • Watch the surfers and the pelicans at four mile beach.
  • Pick apples on Cal Poly's Swanton ranch.
